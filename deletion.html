User Data Deletion – HobbySphere

You can delete your data in one of the following ways:

  1. In-app: Open the app → Settings → Account → Delete Account.
  2. By email: Send an email to spherehobby@gmail.com with the subject “Delete my data” and include the email or user ID used to sign in. We will delete your data within 30 days.

This page satisfies Facebook’s “User Data Deletion” requirement.
